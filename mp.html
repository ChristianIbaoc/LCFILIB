Title: Effective and Affordable Arbovirus Surveillance Using Honey-Baited Nucleic Acid Preservation Cards
Reference Research: Converting Mosquito Surveillance to Arbovirus Surveillance with Honey-Baited Nucleic Acid Preservation Cards

This poster presents a study on integrating FTA cards into mosquito surveillance to address challenges
in traditional arbovirus monitoring, emphasizing cost-effectiveness and sensitivity. The innovative use
of honey-baited FTA cards in low-cost traps enhances virus detection while reducing expenses. Conducted
in Australian urban areas, the study detected 18 viruses, showcasing effectiveness. Utilizing readily
available materials like honey, FTA cards, stockings, milk containers, etc. ensures affordability and
accessibility, facilitating precise surveillance. Continuous monitoring throughout the arbovirus season
enables timely responses, eliminating delays associated with human case-based systems. Additionally, the
details materials used for traps and their costs, highlighting affordability.

Contributors: Christian Ibaoc, Raidon Manaois, Aaron Nicolas, Shawne Tumalad

Title: Baybayin Poster
Reference Research: The Perception of HUMSS on the Inclusion of Baybayin as a National Writing System

This poster showcases the Philippine language script Baybayin. It was made to promote the use of the
language due to the results of a research survey conducted in the Silay Institute which indicated that
HUMMS students recomended the use of Baybayin in society. The poster shows a short description of Baybayin,
the basic rules on how to use it, where to use it, and why to use it. The intended audience of the poster
are highschool students so it was made to be simple as possible.

Contributors: Christian Ibaoc, Raidon Manaois, Aaron Nicolas, Shawne Tumalad

Title: Ang Ekonomiya ng Pinas
Reference Researches:
Bakit bumagsak ang paglago ng ekonomiya sa ikalawang quarter ng 2023?
The Philippine economy under the pandemic: From Asian tiger to sick man again?
Philippine economic recovery in a rebalancing world

Ang aming poster ay tinutukoy tungkol sa mga impormasyon sa ekonomiya ng bansang Pilipinas. Ang tinutukoy
sa aming poster ay ang mga epekto ng pagbagsak ng ating ekonomiya sa ating bansa. Hindi lamang iyon pero
ang mga ibang impormasyon tungkol sa Ekonomiya ng Pilipinas at ang mga mahahalagang pangyayari nito
katulad ng Krisis Pinansyal ng Asia noong 1997, Pandaigdigan Krisis ng 2008 at ang COVID 19 sa Pilipinas
at Timog Silangang Asya, pati ang mga hinaharap ng bansa sa ating ekonomiya sa ngayon. Ang layunin nito
ay ipaalam sa mga tao ang kahalagahan ng Ekonomiyang Pilipino para sa kinabukasan.

Contributors: Sage Rafaelle P. Lejano, Jazmin Abigail R. Zulueta
